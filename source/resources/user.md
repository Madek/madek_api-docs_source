{: #user}
# User `/users/{user-id}`

The `user-id`
parameter can be any of the three unique values `id`, `institutional_id` or the
`email` (in the meaning of email-address) of the user. The parameters require [URL encoding]
in the general case.


{: #get}
## `GET`

Returns the properties of a user.

{: #delete}
## `DELETE`

Returns the properties of a user.


{: #patch}
## PATCH

Updates a user.
